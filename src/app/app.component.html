Technologies (fully decentralised)

The goal is to connect multiple devices—phones, TVs, laptops—together on the one virtual console.

Three distinct technologies enable this:

WebRTC (for peer-to-peer communication) Multiple console emulators (MAME, &etc.) Emscripten (for compiling C/C++ consoles down to web technologies)
